Our Story

A Corner Worth Finding

Sofi's Corner Cafe was born from a simple belief: that good food and good coffee have the power to bring people together. Nestled in the heart of Tacoma, we're your neighborhood spot for a slow morning, a quick lunch, or anything in between.

Everything we serve is made with intention — from our scratch-made waffles to our carefully crafted sandwiches. Come as you are. Stay as long as you like.
