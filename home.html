Our Story

A Corner Worth Finding

Every great corner has a tale, and Sofi's Corner is no exception. Our journey began in December 2022, nestled at 6450 Tacoma Mall Blvd in Tacoma, WA — originally named for Sofia, the beloved daughter of the first owner, a dear and longtime friend of our current proprietor.

While the initial chapter faced its trials, fate had other plans. In February 2024, Sofi's Corner began a new chapter under the stewardship of its current owner — a proud Hispanic, bilingual Mexican-American woman with nearly three decades dedicated to the restaurant industry. Having babysat Sofia since she was a child, the name "Sofi's Corner" resonated deeply. She chose to keep it, making it her own, as a testament to enduring friendship and the continuation of a legacy infused with her own spirit.

This was more than just an acquisition. It was the realization of a long-held dream: a space where every person feels at home, where connections are brewed over an exquisite cup of coffee and a comforting, delicious dish.
